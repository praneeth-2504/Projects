About Us

Welcome to Orphanage Foundation, where we provide shelter, care, and education to orphaned children. Founded with the mission to give every child a loving home, we strive to create a nurturing environment where children can thrive and reach their full potential.

Causes

Here are some of the causes we support:

  • Providing basic needs such as food, clothing, and shelter
  • Ensuring access to quality education and vocational training
  • Offering medical care and emotional support
  • Facilitating adoption and foster care programs
